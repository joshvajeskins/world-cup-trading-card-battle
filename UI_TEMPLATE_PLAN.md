## Chosen Direction

- Production template inspiration: `webgl-studio` / `agency-portfolio` from the catalog for cinematic creative energy.
- MotionSites prompt inspiration: `orbis-nft` for liquid-glass collectible cards, dark field, uppercase display type, and collection grid; `portal` for cinematic bottom-weighted hero composition and staggered blur-fade-up motion.
- Product brand: `X Cup Card Battle`, not a copied template brand.

## First-Screen Judge Moment

The first viewport must show the whole product loop immediately: a full-bleed stadium/card-table hero, three selected card slots, a resolver status strip, and one clear CTA to run the battle demo. Judges should understand "mint cards -> submit lineup -> resolve stats -> battle result" without scrolling.

## Visual System

- Dark pitch-black base with grass-line and floodlight accents.
- Liquid-glass surfaces for card frames and proof panels.
- High-contrast neon green and referee-yellow action accents, with red/blue card-status accents.
- Distinct display type for headings and dense mono text for proof/event details.

## Motion And Interaction

- Staggered blur-fade-up for hero, cards, proof rail, and battle result.
- Hover/tap card lift with stable dimensions.
- Event rail animates newest contract event into view.
- Resolver and fixture data must be visibly labeled as `Admin resolver demo fixture` unless live integration is later proven.

## Reuse / Adaptation Plan

- Adapt `orbis-nft` liquid-glass CSS effect for card and proof surfaces.
- Adapt `portal` hero motion and bottom-weighted content rhythm.
- Do not copy unlicensed sports team/player assets. Use abstract nation-color card art generated in CSS.

## Visual QA Acceptance

- No horizontal overflow at 375 px.
- Hero, selected lineup, battle CTA, and fixture label visible in first viewport at 375, 768, and 1440.
- Card text must not overflow card frames.
- Event/proof rail must remain readable on mobile.
- Local visual QA screenshots are required if formal M2 `/polish` is unavailable.
